What is DisneyBounding?

DisneyBounding is the usage of everyday clothes in the colors and style of popular Disney characters.
As only children under the age of 14 are allowed to wear costumes at the Disney parks, this has become the way for adults to show a little bit of love for their favourite characters, without confusing the visitors of the parks and skirting any legal troubles.
DisneyBounding has become popular thanks to the blog DisneyBound, by Leslie Kay, where they post different outfits in association with a disney character from different mediums.
